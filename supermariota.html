Super Mariota Stadium Android Application


Super Mariota Stadium was a fully-functional Android application I developed in December of 2014. The application is rather simple - it is a side-scrolling platform jumper where the player maneuvers his game character through a map and tries to avoid obstacles. The name is a pun based off the games inspiration, Super Mario, and former University of Oregon quarterback Marcus Mariota.


The game has basic functionality, such as a menu screen and stat keeping of a players high score. The application's development allowed me to gain knowledge and understanding of the Android operating system for further application development.
