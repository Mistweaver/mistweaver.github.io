Engineering

Sense and Avoid with Computer Vision

I personally wrote an application in Python utilizing OpenCV to detect objects in-flight. The application used multiple threads to receive images from a USB camera mounted on the aircraft's wing, provide analysis, and determine any objects that fell within the targeted color hue selected. The entire program was run on a Raspberry Pi 2 mounted in the aircraft's fuselage

Purdue senior design aircraft

Worked in tandem with a team of five other members to design and build a small aircraft from scratch that fit in a budget under $500, could be disassembled into a medium sized plastic bin, assembled in under ten minutes, and carry the necessary equipment for the aforementioned software program. Aircraft underwent three successful test flights.
