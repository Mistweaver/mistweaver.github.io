My name is Bryce Egley.

The greatest tragedy of growing up is learning that dragons do not actually exist. Age robs the world of the magic we viewed through the lens of adolescence. While the rest of my peers set aside such childhood fantasies, I set out to blur the line between my dreams and my reality.

I long aspired to be a professional bum, but I ended up settling in life for studying aerospace engineering at Purdue University. During the course of my studies I became a self-taught software and web developer, and now know my way around a terminal. I am a creator, a writer, an entreprenuer. When that becomes mundane, to pass the time I go out and tackle large, sweaty men (i.e. I go play rugby).

Somewhere between the digital frontier and the tactile terra we occupy exists a more perfect union between two worlds. The advent of artificial intelligence, robotics, the world wide web - all now accessible by billions of individuals through smartphones - has brought about an age unseen in the existence of humanity. And what lies ahead is beyond our wildest imaginations.
